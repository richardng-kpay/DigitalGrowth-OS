# Segments

Audience segment profiles live here. They are shared context for campaign
briefs, content briefs, email briefs, lifecycle work, and Creative Reviewer
checks.

Create one file per meaningful segment using `Templates/segment-profile.md`.
Keep claims tagged with provenance from `Knowledge/Reference/provenance-tags.md`.

## When to add a segment

- A campaign, content piece, or lifecycle flow targets the same audience more
  than once.
- Lark research or customer interviews reveal a stable audience pattern.
- A reviewer says the target audience is too vague.

## Segment quality bar

A useful segment answers:

- Who they are: role, company type, lifecycle stage, or behavior.
- What they need: pain, job-to-be-done, buying trigger, or activation blocker.
- How to reach them: channels, content formats, offers, and objections.
- What to measure: primary KPI and guardrail metric.
- Evidence: source tag, date, and confidence.

## Index rows

Add non-template segment files to `Knowledge/index.md` under a `Segments`
section. If that section does not exist yet, create it during `/wiki-maintain`.
